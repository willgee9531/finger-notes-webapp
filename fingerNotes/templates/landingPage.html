Services

About

  • Welcome to FingerNotes!

    Our goal is to empower students to take control of their learning journey by offering a user-friendly interface that simplifies the organization, retrieval, and sharing of electronic notes. By centralizing all study resources in one place, we aim to streamline the studying process, enhance productivity, and ultimately, improve academic success.

  • Mission Statement

    At FingerNotes, our mission is to revolutionize the way students organize and access their educational materials. We are dedicated to providing a seamless and efficient solution for students to incorporate their class notes, lecture slides, textbooks, and other study materials into one comprehensive software platform.

  • Core Values

    Innovation: We prioritize innovation to continuously improve our software and anticipate students' needs. Reliability: We ensure our software is stable and secure, providing users with uninterrupted access to their enotes.
    Empowerment: Our goal is to empower students to take control of their learning journey by providing intuitive tools and resources.

  • Long Term Goals

    We are committed to continuously innovating and enhancing our software to meet the evolving needs of students in today's digital age. Through collaboration, feedback, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, we strive to become the go-to solution for students seeking a reliable and efficient tool for managing their enotes!

Splash Screen

A modern U.I loading display screen.

While the software loads it's database and interface, this friendly splash screen helps feed the user with a sight-appealing display of both the school name, logo and motto!

Home Screen

Default screen displayed once software loads.

The home screen includes a dynamic image slide show displaying the various school facilities, a welcome note from the school principal and the group of examinations boards and syllabuses offered!

Authentication Screen

A simple authentication screen that permits students with access.

The e-note section starts with this authentication screen that consists of both a registration and login form. The whole idea of this screen is to prevent unauthorized access to the e-note. Next is the term selection screen!

Subject Screen

An array of subjects on the screen.

This screen displays the various subjects for a particular grade with buttons having icons. These buttons are to be pressed once and they open up the slides in the respective presentation package. Students can also logout from this screen!

About Us Screen

Graphical designed information.

On this screen the school's vision, mission and other details are clearly stated with the aid of a graphic design!

Past Question Screen

A clickable button for different past questions.

This screen is a plus to this e-note because students won't be needing any other secondary software or outlet inorder to access past questions for local and international examinations!
